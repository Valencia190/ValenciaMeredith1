Rialto Bridge

Built: 1591

Length: 31.80 metres (104.3 ft)

Address: Sestiere San Polo, 30125 Venezia VE, Italy

Architect: Antonio da Ponte, Antonio Contin

"The Rialto Bridge is the oldest bridge across the canal and is renowned as an architectural and engineering achievement of the Renaissance" (Britannica.com).
