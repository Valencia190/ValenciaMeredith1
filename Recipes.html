Veggies/ Grains

Quinoa Fried Rice

PREP TIME: 10 MINUTES

COOK TIME: 20 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 30 MINUTES

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup dry quinoa
  2. 1.5 cups water
  3. 2-4 tsp low sodium soy sauce add a little to start and extra to taste!
  4. 1-2 cloves garlic minced
  5. 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  6. 1 cup fresh or frozen veggies: peas, carrots, and onion
  7. 1-2 large eggs optional
  8. oil or butter for sautéing (I typically reach for the butter)
  9. salt and pepper to taste

  5. ½ teaspoon ginger
  6. ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
