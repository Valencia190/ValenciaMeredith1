Doge's Palace

Built: 1340

Address: P.za San Marco, 1, 30124 Venezia VE, Italy

Hours: 9am - 6pm

Architects: Andrea Palladio, Antonio Rizzo, Bartolomeo Bon

Doge's Palace was the residence of the Doge of Venice, the former supreme authority of the old republic.
