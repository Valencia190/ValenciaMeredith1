Basilica Di San Marco

Built: 1094

Address: P.za San Marco, 328, 30100 Venezia VE, Italy

Hours: 9:30am - 5:15pm

Architects: Domenico I Contarini

"St Mark's Basilica, is the cathedral church of the Roman Catholic Patriarchate of Venice; it became the episcopal seat of the Patriarch of Venice in 1807, replacing the earlier cathedral of San Pietro di Castello" (wikipedia.org).
