Sarde in Saor

  • Ingredients

  • Sardines
  • Flour
  • Sunflower Oil
  • White Wine
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Sarde in saor are an appetizer based on fried sardines, seasoned with sweet and sour onions, pine nuts and raisins, typical of Venetian cuisine. They are often served as snacks in Venetian bacari. "Saor" means "flavor" in the Venetan.It originated from sailors who wanted to preserve their food during long journeys.

Best Restaurant: All'Arco
