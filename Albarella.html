Albarella


Location: 45010 Isola Albarella, Province of Rovigo, Italy

This 6 km-long sandy beach is lined with attractions such as playgrounds, mini-golf & nightclubs.

Trip Advisor Review: Perfect place on Albarella! Not to crowded and very relaxed atmosphere. Even perfect for small kids. The water is calm and shallow beach/ water.
